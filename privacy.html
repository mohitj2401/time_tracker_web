Privacy Policy for Time Tracker App

Last Updated: October 12, 2023

1. Information We Collect

1.1 No Authentication Required

The Time Tracker App does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use its core features. We do not collect personal identifiers, such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.

1.2 Data Stored in SQLite Database

The Time Tracker App uses SQLite, a local database system, to store the data related to your tasks and activities. This data is stored on your device and is not accessible to us.

1.3 Task Data

The app collects data about your tasks, including their descriptions, start times, end times, and categories. This information is used to generate daily and weekly reports for your convenience.

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Task Data

We use the task data to provide the core functionality of the app, including tracking your time and generating reports. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

2.2 Reports

The app uses your task data to generate reports that help you track your daily and weekly activities by category. These reports are for your personal use and are not shared with third parties.

3. Ads

The Time Tracker App does not include any advertising. You will not be exposed to any third-party ads while using the app.

4. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously. The task data stored on your device is protected with the security features provided by your device's operating system. We do not have access to your data, and we do not store it on external servers.

5. Third-Party Services

The Time Tracker App does not integrate with any third-party services, including social media platforms. Your data is isolated and not shared with external services.

6. Children's Privacy

The Time Tracker App is not designed for or directed at individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us so we can delete it.

7. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes to this policy will be reflected in the updated policy with the last updated date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at [Your Contact Information].

By using the Time Tracker App, you agree to the terms and conditions outlined in this Privacy Policy. Please read this policy carefully and make informed decisions about using our app.
